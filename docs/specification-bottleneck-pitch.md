# The Specification Bottleneck

AI can compress execution. **Specification quality** (defining intent precisely
enough to verify the result) decides whether that produces value or waste.

## The J-Curve

AI cuts productivity before it accelerates it. Average 1.3-point drop in
manufacturing;[^2] some firms fall 60 points before recovery.[^1] Developers
feel faster while measured output falls.[^3] Trough lasts 18-24 months. Early
adopters are climbing out.

## Three Questions

1. **Skill shift.** Specification and verification: core competency or overhead?
2. **Metrics.** We measure build velocity. Do we measure specification quality?
3. **Position.** Where on the J-curve are we, and what moves us through faster?

## The Ask

- **Planning** that puts specification before execution
- **Metrics** that distinguish token drivers from commodity output

30 minutes to map current targets against the bottleneck.
